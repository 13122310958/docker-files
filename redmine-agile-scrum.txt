使用方法：
1.后台增加一个 custom field ,对于issue的。类型为 num , 名字叫 story points.
2.在 trackers中， 点击 summary ，打勾生成的 story points 选项。
效果：在 spring board 中，会以这个值为计算 points.
3.1.在plugin的setting 中。指定 story points 用这个值来计算。
3.2. backlog items choose all
3.3. tasks choose all. 
3.4. field to use with error : choose parent task and story points.
3.5. "sprint board" tab: task status for sprint board: choose all
3.6.  PBI status: choose “new “
	then “save”! 
3.7. go to public a new issue in “sprint board”
3.8. open the new issue , add a “subtask” and set the status in “processing” , than you can see it display in the “processing” tab .

ok! enjoy it.
